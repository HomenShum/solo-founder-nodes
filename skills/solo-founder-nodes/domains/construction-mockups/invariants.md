# Construction Mockups Domain Pack

First wedge for the 3D screenshot scenario.

Jobs:

- brush-select a wall/object/surface,
- replace a material such as brick to wood,
- preserve windows, doors, wall dimensions, and room structure,
- export/reopen a reviewable mockup.

Blocking gates:

- selected-region proof,
- material-replacement proof,
- structure-preservation proof,
- before/after visual proof,
- export/reopen proof.

Operation RALPH should cover `construction.brush-select-wall`,
`construction.replace-wall-material`, and `construction.export-mockup`.
